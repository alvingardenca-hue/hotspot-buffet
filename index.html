FAQs

Have questions before visiting? Here’s what most people ask.

When are the $24.99 special hours?
Buffet pricing starts from $24.99 during select lunch and late-night hours.

Lunch specials:
Mon–Fri · 11:30 AM–3 PM

Late-night specials:
Daily · 10:30 PM–1 AM

Do I need a reservation?

Walk-ins are welcome. Reservations are recommended during busy dinner hours, especially from 6:30 PM to 9:30 PM.

What is the maximum group size?

Groups of up to 10 people can usually be seated together at one table.

Is there a time limit?

Yes. The standard dining time limit is 120 minutes.

Can I have BBQ alone?

BBQ requires a minimum of 2 people. For solo diners, hot pot is available.

Can I choose BBQ or hot pot only?

Yes. You can choose BBQ or hot pot. If you want both, hot pot can be added for $2.99 per person.

Do you serve alcohol?

Yes. We serve beer and other drinks, which makes Hot Spot Buffet a popular late-night dining option.

Do you offer kids pricing?

Yes. Kids under 4 eat free, and children aged 5 to 9 get 50% off.

Is parking available?

Yes. Parking is available right in front of the restaurant, making it convenient for groups and late-night visits.
